Erick "Aspas" Santos.

Erick "aspas" Santos isn't just a VALORANT player—he's a living legend. From the moment he stepped onto the competitive scene, he redefined what it means to dominate a server. His aim is surgical, his movements are calculated, and his presence in-game is pure artistry.
